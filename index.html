~Instructions~


  • You will be given a number at the start of the game
  • There are four ChuChus below. By clicking on a ChuChu, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score!
  • You Win the game when your total score matches the number you were given at the start of the game. If your score goes above that number, you lose.
  • Each ChuChu's value is hidden at the start of every game until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the value of each ChuChu changes! Good luck :)
